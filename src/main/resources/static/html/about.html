What is Logo? Logo is a platform for people, to share what they know or read, and get the information verified, according to the source the information is coming from. Posted by people, verified by people.
Why is Logo needed? Journalism these days, are only targeting views and ratings. Especially in digital media, where everything can be written freely but, sometimes with sided opinion which is really bad for the information itself. Logo itselfs also is an opinion of person who thinks that journalism has lost its purpose, which is to deliver the real and truthful information without spicing up.
Who created Logo? Logo is created by a student, who is going hard against hoax in the digital media, and who has something against journalism these days, where rating is the most important thing instead of truthful information without any sided opinion.
When is Logo created? Remember CoVid-19?? That is when Logo is first developed by a student in quarantine.
How does Logo work? When a person shares an information on a thread, they are required to include the source they get the information from. From the source, people or reader has to vote, whether the particular post is legit or whether it is a hoax, of course not according to one's OPINION, but according to facts why the information is not legit with source.
